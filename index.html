Digital Well-being Platform - Debug Mental Health
•Debug Mental Health is a web platform developed as a submission for the CodeFury 6.0 Hackathon.Developed in under 10 hours.
•Team Members: Supradeep R , Sai Uttej R , Saketh V, Akshay R, Pavan Kumar S
•This platform is dedicated to fostering mental well-being and raising awareness about mental health issues in the digital age.
•Users can create accounts, write personal journals, engage with the community journal, play relaxing games, listen to calming music, access mental health resources, and find inspiration through a curated collection of quotes.
•We integrated external APIs such as Quotable for quotes and Spotify for music.
•Built with React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Firebase for backend functionality.
